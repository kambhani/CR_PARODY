ARCHERS

WHY IT IS OVERPOWERED

Archers are an extremely powerful card, indicated by their inclusion in one of the most popular (and frankly one of the best) decks in the game: 2.9 X-Bow. The card has enough hitpoints to withstand a Zap, Snowball, Barbarian Barrel, and even The Log. Paired with a tank like Ice Golem or Knight, the duo can make short work of nearly any troop. Archers can also counter Balloon and Graveyard, two common win conditions. The range of this card only makes it more annoying to deal with. This card needs a nerf to make it more fair to players who do not play 2.9 X-Bow. The best plan would be to nerf HP to 216 so that the card dies to The Log. This will make the card (and 2.9 X-Bow) easier to deal with, thus improving the meta.

WHY IT IS UNDERPOWERED

Archers see a below average use rate because they are simply not that good. It is outclassed by cards like Firecracker (has splash), Musketeer (harder to spell + more DPS), and Mega Minion (flies + more DPS). The card is also rarely seen outside of 2.9 X-Bow. Being viable in only one specific deck means that the card lacks much versatility. Buffing the card could help with that. Specifically, a range increase to 6 tiles will allow the card to better compete with Musketeer. A DPS increase of 5% could help make the card viable in more decks. These changes could bring an iconic card from Clash of Clans into the Clash Royale spotlight.
