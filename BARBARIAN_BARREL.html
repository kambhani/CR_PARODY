BARBARIAN BARREL

WHY IT IS OVERPOWERED

Simply put, Barbarian Barrel's two-in-one functionality of spell and troop makes it far too powerful. The spell portion can make short work of swarms like Skeleton Army, Goblin Gang, Goblin Barrel, etc. The troop that spawns can be used as a tank (for cards like Graveyard and Three Musketeers) and damage (cycle at bridge for tower chip damage). The inherent flexibility of this card is seen in its very high use rates in Grand Challenges. To bring this card in line with other spells, nerfs are needed. One such nerf could be to reduce the travel distance of the spell so that the Barbarian will not reach the tower when the card is cycled at the bridge. Another reasonable nerf would be to reduce the damage so the card does not kill Goblins.

WHY IT IS UNDERPOWERED

While the Barbarian Barrel may seem good in theory, in reality it is outshined by The Log and Zap, meaning that the card is not a viable contender. Its travel distance is much smaller than The Log's and unlike Zap, this card does not attack aerial units or reset troops. The spawned Barbarian also dies very quickly, meaning that the tanking properties it possesses are minimal. Overall, this card desparately needs buff to put it back into the metagame. The best buff, in my opinion, would be to increase its damage so it kills Archers and Firecrackers. This will give the card a distinct advantage over The Log and Zap, meaning use rates will go up. If the aforementioned change comes to no avail, a travel distance buff could also be employed.
