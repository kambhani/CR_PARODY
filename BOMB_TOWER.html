BOMB TOWER

WHY IT IS OVERPOWERED

Bomb Tower is one of the most powerful buildings in the game, and for just four elixir, it seems a little too powerful. It hard counters win conditions like Royal Hogs and Elixir Golem, and its splash damage allows the card to decimate bait decks. Despite only being able to target ground troops, the card can actually counter air cards like the Balloon because its HP allows the Princess Towers to finish the aerial cards off. Bomb Tower can be found in decks ranging from Splashyard to Miner control. Overall, this card needs a nerf. Some nerfs which could make the card less powerful include an HP nerf, a damage nerf, or both. Bomb Tower is a toxic card that has no need to be as prevalant as it is.

WHY IT IS UNDERPOWERED

Bomb Tower is one of the weaker buildings in the game. Its short lifetime of 25 seconds means that the card gets little value. Decks that this card supposedly hard counters, like Royal Hog decks, can bypass this issue by carrying Earthquake. It can only attack ground troops, making the card virtually useless against Lava Hound decks. The death damage that this card provides is small. Additionally, most troops walk out of the death bomb radius by the time it explodes. Finally, the DPS of this card is rather absymal. In sum, Bomb Tower needs a buff. Some possible buffs include: a lifetime increase, a DPS buff, or both. Bringing this card back into the metagame will be a good thing.
