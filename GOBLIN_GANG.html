GOBLIN GANG

WHY IT IS OVERPOWERED

The game states that Goblin Gang is a value pack, but what it does not realize is that the value the card provides is simply too much. It is a swarm that can take out a vast majority of the troops in the game, even some surprising ones. For example, the card can take out a naked Valkyrie with the help of the crown tower. The card can surround troops like Firecracker, Wizard, etc. to take them out. Unlike Skeleton Army, this card can take out aerial troops like Mega Minion. Another benefit that this card possesses over Skeleton Army is that the swarm is not completely zappable, meaning that this swarn is more resilient. This resilence becomes especially important against decks like PEKKA Bridgespam, which use Zap. Goblin Gang is a very good swarm, as shown by its position as one of the most used swarms in the game, right after Skeletons and Bats. Hence, this card could use nerfs to make it more fair. These include a count reduction (like 2 and 2) and/or a spawn pattern change (have Spear Goblins spawn in front of Goblins). If the above changes were to be implemented, then Goblin Gang would no longer be so oppressive.

WHY IT IS UNDERPOWERED

Goblin Gang is quite underwhelming, being only viable in bait decks. Additionally, this card is easily countered by small spells. Killing the card is easily, especially with cards like Valkyrie and Wizard. This has the additional effect of setting up a counterpush, which could be deadly. Using the card on defense is risky if the opponent uses a prediction spell. The card does far less DPS than Skeleton Army, a card which also has the same elixir cost. For this reason, Skeleton Army is used in non-bait decks like Giant Graveyard. The real problem, though, exists with the design of the card. Other cards do its jobs better. For DPS, there is Skeleton Army, as stated earlier. For cheap ground swarm, there are Skeletons and Goblins. For cheap aerial defense, there are Spear Goblins and Firecracker. Being a "value pack" is just a sugercoated way of saying "mediocre at everything". To remedy this situation, the card needs buffs. The best buff would be to revert the card to its earlier state and have three Spear Goblins. This will make it good enough to use more widely in the meta.
