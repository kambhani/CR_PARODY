SKELETONS

WHY IT IS OVERPOWERED

Consistently one of the most used cards in the game, Skeletons provide far too much value for one elixir. With the help of the crown tower, they can take out cards like Mega Minion, Mini Pekka, and Ice Wizard for insane positive elixir trades. Left alone, this card can dish out over 200 DPS at tournament standard which is far too much. They are also good for cycling. Their immense utility explains why they fit in a variety of decks, including 2.6 Hog cycle, 2.9 X-Bow cycle, 3.5 Ice Bow, and 2.8 Log Bait. Clearly, this card is in need of a nerf. Some ways to balance this card (while not harming other skeleton-based cards) include reducing the number of skeletons to 2 and making the skeletons spawn in a line (this will prevent skeletons from surrounding troops). These changes will make the card less toxic.

WHY IT IS UNDERPOWERED

Skeletons are quite overrated, despite their high use rates. They die to anything and cannot be used as solo tower chip damage. Furthermore, you can get much more value by using Skeleton Army, which give a skeleton for 0.2 elixir, compared to the 0.33 elixir per skeleton in Skeletons. This is unfair. Skeletons is also a high-skill card, meaning that lower-level players cannot use it as effectively. To remedy this abhorrent situation, the card must be given meaningful buffs. One such buff would be to spawn four skeletons in a square formation. This allows the card to better surround solo troops coming to the tower. It also decreases the cost per skeleton to 0.25 elixir which is much more reasonable. Overall, skeletons are almost at the point of being good. They just need a little more love, and I hope that they receive it.
