ICE SPIRIT

WHY IT IS OVERPOWERED

Ice Spirit has been around for a long time, and in my opinion, it feels quite overused. Acting as basically a mini-freeze for one-fourth the normal cost, its versatility is astounding. It retargets troops, can be used as a cycle card, can be used as chip damage, and distracts/freezes enemy troops. For one elixir, that is too much. Nerfs which can bring it in line with other troops include an HP nerf, a reduction in freeze time (to about 0.1s), and complete removal of its retarget ability. These changes will make the card more fair.

WHY IT IS UNDERPOWERED

Honestly, ice spirit has been very underwhelming, seeing as it has so much potential. It suffers the injustice of being the least-used one elixir card in the game. This is unacceptable. Its splash radius is also pitiful, being next to useless in dealing with skeleton army and other mass swarms. The card's freeze duration is pathetic, and honestly, it is next to useless. To bring Ice Spirit back into the metagame, I propose the following buffs: increase splash radius by 50%, increase HP by 10%, and double the freeze duration. These changes may just make the card viable again.
