DART GOBLIN

WHY IT IS OVERPOWERED

Dart Goblin is too overpowered for its cost of three elixir. For one, its range enables it to out-range many building like Cannon and Inferno Tower. This is unfair. The range of the card also allows it to snipe many buildings placed on the opponent's side of the field. In a push, the card can stay a safe distance behind the tank because of its range, preventing splash units from easily getting rid of it. Dart Goblin is also useful bait and is seen in 2.8 Double Barrel Bait and 3.8 Prince Log Bait. Another annoying thing about the card is its DPS of over 140. This is insane and it allows the card to take out massive pushes when tanked for. The fast attack rate also means that the card can make short work of Graveyard. Finally, the extremely fast speed of this card makes reacting to it is very hard. The card can take off a lot of tower damage when bridge spammed, often because the opponent simply cannot react to it. In sum, Dart Goblin needs a nerf. The most needed one would be a range reduction to six tiles as this would allow the card to be targetted by Inferno Tower. Another crucial nerf that the card needs is a speed nerf as this would allow the opponent to more easily react to the card.

WHY IT IS UNDERPOWERED

Dart Goblin has below-average use rates and the reason is clear as day â€” in its current form, the card is too weak. Arguably the biggest problem with the card is its fragility. While this is intended, the card could use a little love in this regard. Another issue is range. In several aspects, Magic Archer outclasses this card, most notable in range. The additionally 0.5 tiles Magic Archer gets means that many people who would use Dart Goblin for the range now use Magic Archer instead. Clearly, the card needs buffs in the two areas mentioned earlier. In regards to the first issue, an HP increase to 230 would still make the card loggable while giving it a small buff. The card would still be used in bait decks because of this. In regards to the second issue, a range increase to seven tiles, similar to the Magic Archer, should help gain the card some usage. These changes will help bring an underused card into the spotlight.
